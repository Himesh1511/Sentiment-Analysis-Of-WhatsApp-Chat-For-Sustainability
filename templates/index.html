WhatsApp Chat Analysis Tool

Gain insights from your WhatsApp chats with comprehensive analysis and visualizations.

Step 1: Export Your WhatsApp Chat

To get started, you need to export your WhatsApp chat. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the chat you want to export in WhatsApp.
  2. Tap on the three dots in the upper-right corner to open the menu.
  3. Select 'More' and then 'Export chat'.
  4. Choose whether to include media files or not.
  5. Save the exported chat file (usually in .txt format) to your device.

Step 2: Upload Your Chat File

Once you have your exported chat file, you can upload it for analysis:

  1. Click the 'Choose File' button to select your exported chat file.
  2. Click the 'Upload' button to submit the file.
  3. Wait for the analysis to complete and view your results.

What to Expect from the Analysis

Our analysis provides detailed insights into your chat data:

  • Message Frequency by Sender: Visualize how many messages each participant has sent.
  • Sentiment Analysis: View the distribution of positive, negative, and neutral messages.
  • Formality Analysis: See whether messages are formal or informal.
  • Texts Per Time Period: Examine the number of texts sent daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly.
  • Summary Statistics: Get key metrics like the number of unique senders and the total number of messages.
